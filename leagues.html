Leagues

Crestwood Country Club offers a variety of different golf leagues for members to participate in. Leagues are a great way to meet new people and we highly encourage people to get involved in our numberous leagues. Note that golf leagues are for members only. If you are a member and would like to inquire extra information about golf leagues please call the pro shop at (620)-231-6530. Annual leagues at Crestwood include

Mens League

Held on Wednesday evenings throughout the summer, Men's league is a great way to come out, have a few drinks, and enjoy a little friendly competition. The format is 2-man best ball match play, with each team gaining 0.5 points for each hole that they win. Entry fee is $40 and an active handicap is required.

Ladies League

Ladies League is held on Thursday mornings and late evenings for the entire summer and part of the fall. The Crestwood Women's Golf Association decides the formats that will be used each week. Ladies league a great way to get out, play golf with new people, and enjoy our wonderful golf course.

Couples League

Couples league is a great way for you and your spouse to enjoy one another's company while playing golf. It takes place on Sunday evenings throughout the summer. Couples are paired up in 6somes, with 3 couples per team, and play a scotch scramble format. Couples are given points each week for wins and an overall tally is found at the end of the league
