Membership

There has never been a better time to become a member at Crestwood! Full memberships include all of the amenities and unlimited golf. If you want to join but aren't interested in the golf aspect then a social membership is available as well! Membership contracts are 24 month contracts and dues are billed monthly. Membership information for each of the different membership types and associated charge can be found in the table below. All prices below are monthly prices.

Membership type Price/mo Food minimum Unlimited cart usage
Full: 18-29 years old $100 $0 $60
Full: 30-49 years old $150 $25 $60
Full: 50+ years old $175 $25 $60
Family $250 $75 $60
Social $100 $100 n/a

Member for a day

If you would like to experience Crestwood before committing to a membership then take advantage of our Member for a Day offer. With this offer you would get to experience all of the features that comes with a membership, including a complimentary round of golf on any weekday of your choosing! to inquire about trying out our member for a day offer please call the pro shot at (620)-231-6530.
