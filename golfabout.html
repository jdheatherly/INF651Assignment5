Golf Course

The golf course at Crestwood originally debuted in 1960 as a nine hole course. The course was re-designed and expanded to 18 holes in 1978 by Kansas Golf Hall Of Fame architect Floyd Farley. The layout features four sets of tees for men and two for ladies or juniors to meet every golfers skill level. Some of the key features of the course include

  • Par 72

  • 6700 yards (back tees)

  • Zoysia fairways

  • Bent greens

  • 11 holes with water coming into play

  • 37 sand traps

  • Immense native grass
