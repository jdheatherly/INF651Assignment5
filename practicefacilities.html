Practice Facilities

Driving Range

Our driving range is a full-sized range that is perfect for working on your swing. Golf crates are set out daily so you don't have to worry about coming to the pro shop and getting range balls. 6 small target greens are scattered throughout the range at different distances so you have a target to aim at for every club in your bag.

Short Game Area

The short game area located by the cart shed offers a range of different short game shots. Practice your short game from even or uneven lies, from the fairway, rough, or the bunker. On the course you see a multitude of different shots around the greens and this is the perfect place to practice those shots. The short game putting green includes undulations aplenty so you can practice your breaking putts here as well.

Putting Green

Getting ready to head out on the course and want to hit a few putts before stepping on the 1st tee? If so, our putting green has you covered. Located in front of legends and close to the 1st tee, the putting green is a convenient place to roll a few before heading out for your round.
