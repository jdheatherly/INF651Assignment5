Welcome To Crestwood Country Club

Crestwood Country Club is Crawford County's premier country club experience. The club was founded in 1958 and provides fun for the entire family. Crestwood has that small-town charm and puts an emphasis on family and community.

Crestwood offers amenities for all ages. We offer a pristine, challenging 18 hole golf course that will leave you wanting to play more. Legends Snack Bar is open for your pre, post, or mid-round food and snacks. Our practice facilities offer golfers the perfect place to master all aspects of the game. Our dining area is vast and accompanied by an indoor/outdoor pavillion area. This space makes for a great place to come have dinner, have a drink, or even host a wedding or outing.
Crestwood truly is a wonderful experience, and we hope that you will consider joining our community. Please feel free to explore all Crestwood has to offer via this website. Navigation at the top, along with the linked image gallery below can be used to navigate.
