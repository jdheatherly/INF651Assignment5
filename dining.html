Dining

Crestwood Country Club is the perfect place for an enjoyable dining experience. Head chef John Fields and his culinary team make some of the tastiest plates and dishes in the area. Our waiting staff is upbeat, friendly, and ready to add to your experience. The kitchen is open Wednesday through Sunday so the dining area follows the same schedule. The dining area upstairs is the perfect place to have lunch or dinner with the family. Also, if you are looking to come out and watch a Chiefs game you can enjoy the bar area. Breakfast is offered on Sundays and daily food specials are always available. Come hungry and you won't leave disappointed.

  • Days open: Wednesday through Sunday
  • Hours of Operation: 8am - 9pm (summer). Closing time adjusts with darkness.
